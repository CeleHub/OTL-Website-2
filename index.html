Features

Wholesales

At Obijons Trade Link Limited, we specialize in wholesale distribution of motor spare parts, offering both imported and locally manufactured products. Our extensive experience in the industry has enabled us to build a strong network of dealers, both within Nigeria and internationally. We provide high-quality motor parts from our registered OTL brand, which is known for its exceptional durability and longevity. While we primarily serve wholesale clients, we are always open to expanding our network and partnerships. We ensure reliable delivery services and are committed to supporting our clients with product expertise and guidance whenever needed.

Retail

In addition to our wholesale services, Obijons Trade Link Limited also offers retail sales for individual customers. We provide a wide range of motor spare parts, including those from our trusted OTL brand, which is renowned for its durability and long-lasting performance. Whether you're a car owner looking for a specific part or someone in need of expert advice, we are here to assist. While our primary focus is on wholesale distribution, we ensure that our retail customers receive the same level of quality, reliability, and customer care that we offer to our business partners. We also provide trusted recommendations for trusted installation services when needed.

Consulting

At Obijons Trade Link Limited, we not only serve wholesale clients but also cater to retail customers. Whether you’re a car owner looking for specific parts or someone seeking expert advice, we are here to assist. As part of our commitment to customer satisfaction, we offer free consultations, helping you find the right parts and solutions for your vehicle. Additionally, we provide trusted recommendations for installation services when needed.
